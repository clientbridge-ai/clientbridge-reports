Channel-by-Channel Strategy

📍

Google Business Profile (GBP) — Two Separate Listings

Free

Each location must have its own verified, fully completed GBP. This directly controls your appearance in the Google Maps 3-Pack — where 40–60% of local med spa bookings originate. A complete GBP typically drives a 70% increase in direction requests and calls within 60 days of optimization.

Rainey Street GBP Name: "La Vie Med Spas — Rainey Street"
Primary category: Medical Spa
Secondary: Day Spa, Beauty Salon
Add: Evening hours, event space, walk-in availability
Photos: 25+ (bar/street vibe, treatment rooms, before/afters)
Posts: Weekly — specials, events, featured services
Oakland Ave GBP Name: "La Vie Med Spas — Oakland / Clarksville"
Primary category: Medical Spa
Secondary: Wellness Center, Day Spa
Add: Membership info, parking, quiet neighborhood
Photos: 25+ (serene, residential feel, team photos)
Posts: Weekly — wellness tips, membership offers, seasonal
  • Add every service to both GBP listings individually — this feeds Google's local service search filters
  • Enable the MindBody booking link as the primary "Book" action on both profiles
  • Set up automated post-visit review requests via MindBody or a service like Podium
  • Target 5+ new Google reviews per month per location
🔍

Google Local Services Ads (LSAs)

$800–$1,500/mo per location

LSAs appear at the very top of Google search results — above regular ads and above organic results. They show your business name, review rating, and a direct call/message button. You pay per lead (phone call or message), not per click. Med spas and wellness businesses are eligible. This is the fastest paid channel for driving booked appointments from search intent.

  • Set up one LSA campaign per location with separate phone numbers to track lead attribution
  • High-priority service categories to bid on: Botox/injectables, facials, IV therapy, weight loss (Semaglutide), massage
  • Respond to every LSA lead within 5 minutes — response speed directly affects your LSA ranking
  • Dispute any leads that are not genuine appointment inquiries to keep cost-per-lead low
Rainey Street — LSA Focus Bid higher on evening/weekend times. Prioritize: injectables, facials, event packages, walk-in availability messaging.
Oakland — LSA Focus Bid on recurring treatment terms. Prioritize: membership, weight loss, IV therapy, wellness packages.
💻

Google Search Ads (PPC)

$1,000–$2,500/mo total

Paid search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords where organic rankings aren't yet established. Particularly valuable while SEO fixes from the audit are taking effect (typically 60–120 days to see ranking movement).

  • Campaign 1 — Injectables: "botox austin tx," "lip filler austin," "dysport near me," "botox price austin" — bid for both locations
  • Campaign 2 — Wellness: "semaglutide austin tx," "IV therapy austin," "weight loss injections austin"
  • Campaign 3 — Spa/Facial: "hydrafacial austin," "med spa austin," "facial near me austin"
  • Use location extensions to show each address and enable call extensions
  • Send traffic to location-specific landing pages (not the homepage) — create one per location if not already live
  • Set a 5-mile radius geo-fence around each location for all campaigns
📸

Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook)

$1,500–$3,000/mo total

The highest-visual-impact channel for a beauty brand. Instagram is where Austin's med spa clientele discovers new providers. Meta's geo-targeting by radius is ideal for a two-location setup — you can run location-specific creative that resonates with each neighborhood's audience.

Rainey Street — Meta Strategy Target: Women 25–42, 2-mile radius, interests: nightlife, beauty, self-care, Rainey Street. Ad creative: "Pre-night-out lip flip?" / "Treat yourself before the weekend." CTA: Book same-day appointment.
Oakland — Meta Strategy Target: Women 30–55, 3-mile radius, interests: wellness, skincare, fitness, home ownership. Ad creative: "Your neighborhood wellness ritual." / "Clarksville's hidden gem." CTA: Join VIP membership.
  • Retargeting campaign: Anyone who visits lvmedspas.com and doesn't book sees follow-up ads for 30 days
  • Lookalike audiences: Upload your existing client list from MindBody and create lookalike audiences in Meta Ads Manager — your highest-quality prospecting tool
  • Seasonal promotions: Valentine's Day couples packages, summer body sculpting, holiday gift cards — plan 4–6 weeks ahead
  • Content format: Reels and carousel ads consistently outperform static images for beauty brands — invest in monthly content shoots at both locations
  • Instagram @lavieenrainey: Ensure organic posts and paid ads feel cohesive — boosting existing top-performing organic posts is a quick win
🌱

Local SEO — Two-Location Pages + Content

$500–$1,000/mo (agency or contractor)

Building on the audit findings, organic search is the long-term engine that compounds over time. These actions directly support booking volume at both locations.

  • Create two dedicated location pages: /rainey-street-med-spa/ and /clarksville-med-spa/ — each with unique content, embedded Google Map, staff associated with that location, and a location-specific booking CTA
  • Optimize all service pages with geo-modifiers ("in Austin, TX" / "at our Rainey Street location")
  • Create a Botox/injectables landing page — the highest-volume med spa keyword in Austin, currently missing
  • Create a Semaglutide/weight loss page — fastest-growing med spa search category nationally
  • Build citations for both locations on Yelp, Healthgrades, RealSelf, Vagaro, Mindbody marketplace, and Zocdoc
  • Publish 2 blog posts/month targeting educational queries: "how much does Botox cost in Austin," "what is a HydraFacial," "Semaglutide vs Ozempic for weight loss Austin"
✉️

Email + SMS — Reactivation & Retention

$200–$400/mo (tool cost)

Your existing client list in MindBody is one of your most valuable assets. Reactivating lapsed clients costs a fraction of acquiring new ones. Consistent email and SMS keeps La Vie top-of-mind between visits and drives repeat bookings.

  • Win-back campaign: Clients who haven't booked in 90+ days get a personalized "We miss you" offer (10–15% off next visit)
  • Monthly email newsletter: New services, seasonal specials, practitioner spotlights, wellness tips — one per month, location-segmented
  • Post-visit SMS: 3 days after appointment — thank you + review request link. 30 days after — "Ready for your next visit? Book now."
  • Birthday campaigns: Birthday month offer sent 2 weeks before client's birthday — consistently high open and conversion rates
  • Membership upsell flow: After 2nd visit, automated email introducing the VIP membership with ROI breakdown ("Members save an average of $X/month")
🤝

Referral Program + Local Partnerships

$300–$600/mo

Word-of-mouth is the #1 discovery channel for med spas. A structured referral program turns your happiest clients into your best marketers. Local partnerships extend reach into both neighborhoods at minimal cost.

Rainey Street Partnerships

  • Partner with Rainey Street bars/restaurants on "pre-night-out" packages
  • Connect with nearby hotels (Marriott, Homewood Suites on Rainey) for guest referrals
  • Offer bachelorette party packages — Rainey is a top ATX bachelorette destination
  • Cross-promote with neighboring fitness studios (yoga, pilates)

Oakland / Clarksville Partnerships

  • Connect with Clarksville-area physicians, OBGYNs, and dermatologists for referrals
  • Partner with luxury real estate agents in Old West Austin for client gifting
  • Outreach to local schools/PTA groups for group spa day packages
  • Cross-promote with local boutiques and wine bars in the neighborhood
  • Referral program structure: Referring client gets $25–$50 credit; new client gets 15% off first visit. Track via MindBody referral source field.
  • Share referral links on post-visit receipts, email signatures, and thank-you SMS messages
