Engagement Plan

Content without engagement is a monologue. Here's how Kevin turns posts into conversations and conversations into relationships.

On Posting Day

  • First 60 minutes: Respond to every comment personally. Don't just like — reply with a sentence or a follow-up question. First-hour activity is LinkedIn's #1 distribution signal.
  • Agree and build: When someone disagrees, find common ground first. "That's fair — here's where I'd push back slightly…" drives more conversation than defending your take.
  • Feature the best comment: Reply to the most interesting response with a highlighted response. This rewards participation and signals to others that commenting is worth their time.

Between Posts (3x Per Week)

  • Comment on 5 posts per day from people in the LO/mortgage space. Real comments, not emoji. This grows Kevin's visibility in adjacent networks without publishing anything new.
  • DM 2 people per week who engaged meaningfully with a post — not to pitch, to continue the conversation. These become the referral relationships.
  • Repurpose strong comments into future post ideas. When a commenter surfaces an insight worth building on, note it. The best posts often come from what your audience says back.

Monthly Metrics to Track

  • Engagement rate target: 2–4% (comments + reactions + shares ÷ impressions) — strong for professional services content
  • Comments per post: 10+ signals a high-performing post worth repurposing or expanding into a carousel
  • Profile views: Should increase 15–25% month-over-month as content volume builds consistently
  • Inbound DMs referencing specific posts: Track these — they're the highest-quality leads in the funnel
