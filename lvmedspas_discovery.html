01 — Executive Summary

The Opportunity is Large. The Funnel is Broken.

LVS Med Spas has two Austin locations with solid brand recognition and real search demand — but significant revenue is leaking at every stage of the digital funnel. 7,319 people viewed their GBP profiles and generated zero bookings. The website is visible in 53,095 monthly searches but converts only 1.65% to clicks. Of 6,418 website sessions, only 46 resulted in a real business action — 36 phone call taps and 10 booking flow entries — a true conversion rate of 0.72%. GA4 had previously inflated this figure to 5.9% by counting auto-collected noise events as conversions. Organic search sends the highest quality traffic but represents just 19% of sessions. Competitors like Viva Day Spa are generating 11× more organic traffic value from the same keyword universe.

02 Redirect /sxsw to homepage or a relevant service page — stop sending 1,000+ monthly visitors to a dead end
03 Fix Unassigned traffic (742 sessions, 7% engagement) — likely a UTM or GA4 configuration issue losing attribution

06 Push deep tissue massage to page 1 — currently position 20, one content improvement from a major traffic gain

🗓️ Medium Term — 60-90 Days
